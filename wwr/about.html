River Adventure

In this crazy world it can be hard to get our friends and family to step away from their phones long enough to have a conversation, let alone build meaningful relationships. River Adventure seeks to create opportunities for teamwork, communication and fun, all of which culminate into memories that will last forever! At River Adventure, Finding Smiles is Our Goal!

History

We have families and we have struggled with the disconnect that modern technology has created. We noticed that we were losing contact with our children and we wanted to create bonds that would last forever. What better way to build family connections, than working together to traverse the powerful currents of rivers! We used to be sad and bored, then we discovered white water rafting. Now we are happy and adventurous. Join us and find your smile!
